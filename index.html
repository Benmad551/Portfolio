IT Support Stories

Here are some of my personal stories relating to tech and my IT support:

The beeping UPS

Problem: The UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply) for the register system in a retail store was beeping continuously. The register system was still operational, but the beeping was distracting for both employees and customers.
What I did: I first looked at the UPS itself to see if there was something that looked off. After looking at that, it looked like there was no power going into the UPS so I checked the plug and saw that it was starting to fall out of the socket. After pushing it in, the beeping stopped.
Takeaway: Start troubleshooting with the small things first.

Locked PC

Problem: Help needed setting up a used PC that was locked in the previous owner's account.
What I did: I researched tutorials online for account recovery, and after going through different methods, I ended up resetting the PC to factory settings for the user to use a new account.
Takeaway: Sometimes you need to stop and take the simple route. I wanted to recover the old account, but it was easier to reset the PC.
